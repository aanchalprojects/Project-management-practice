# Risk Register

This document identifies potential project risks, their impact, probability, and mitigation strategies.

| Risk ID | Risk Description                       | Impact (High/Med/Low) | Probability (High/Med/Low) | Mitigation Strategy                          |
|---------|---------------------------------------|----------------------|----------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| R1      | Delay in stakeholder approvals        | High                 | Medium                     | Set clear deadlines and reminders          |
| R2      | Resource unavailability               | High                 | Medium                     | Maintain backup resource plan               |
| R3      | Scope creep                            | Medium               | High                       | Follow formal change control process       |
| R4      | Budget overrun                         | High                 | Medium                     | Monitor costs regularly, track variances   |
| R5      | Quality issues                         | Medium               | Medium                     | Conduct regular QA reviews and audits      |

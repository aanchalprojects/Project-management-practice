# Quality Management Plan

## Purpose
Ensure the project deliverables meet defined quality standards and stakeholder expectations.

## Quality Objectives
- Deliver documentation without errors  
- Follow PMBOK best practices  
- Maintain schedule and cost accuracy

## Quality Assurance Activities
- Peer reviews of all documents  
- Regular QA audits  
- Verification of task completion as per WBS

## Quality Control Metrics
- Number of errors in deliverables  
- On-time completion of tasks  
- Adherence to PM processes
